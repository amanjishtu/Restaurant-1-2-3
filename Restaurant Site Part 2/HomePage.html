The Pavilion
Restaurant is located in the centre of
Fitzroy Gardens next to the Tudor Village
and Fairies tree.
The Original kiosk was opened in the centre
of the Gardens in 1908. This half-timber style
building is first such facility in the public
park. and appears to have been an immediate success.
